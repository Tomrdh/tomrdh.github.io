Interaction Rules

The best place to find what you can and can't do here. Read them first, if you want to avoid being punished!

Global Rules

Please note that these rules are applied to everything related to my content.
  1. ALWAYS stay respectful, so we can have a nice friendly community. The owner and moderators are not responsible of any bad behaviors.
  2. All platforms have their own rules, terms and guidelines. Follow them depending on where you are.
  3. Don't spam, flood nor anything like this anywhere.
  4. Advertising is strictly prohibited, except on allowed places specially made for this. Marketing is also not allowed.
  5. This community is not made for discrimination (e.g. racism, homophobia, …) and debates.
  6. Your profile mustn't be inappropriate, including your profile picture, your nickname, your status and all the content that everyone can see.
  7. If something has a specific topic (like a #discord-channel), don't go off-topic. Speak about that topic or go somewhere else.
  8. There are children everywhere. Never send, show or redirect to any adult content (e.g. porn, gore, NSFW, 18+, …).
  9. Links are allowed, but malicious or untrusted ones will be instantly deleted.
  10. Never share any personal or private information. We don't need this.
  11. Fake or troll accounts will be instantly banned.

Rules on Discord Addons

These rules are applied only on Discord Addons, and when you send a DM to the owner.
  1. All global rules are applied.
  2. Most of them are written directly in the server, check the #rules channel. Some rules may appear twice.
  3. If you have any life problem (like depression, …) and want to talk about it, please go to Direct Messages (DMs). Fake situations are a waste of time and is not tolerated. If noticed, you'll be instantly blocked from DMs and limited from accessing the server.
